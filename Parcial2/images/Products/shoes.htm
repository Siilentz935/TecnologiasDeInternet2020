Product Details

Your game can't hide in the shadows with the first ever racquetball shoe from Babolat. The Babolat Shadow Tour Racquetball Shoes grace the scale at 12.8 ounces and feature a Michelin gum rubber outsole for superior court traction. Mesh surrounding the midsole keeps your foot as cool as an evening breeze, while a leather upper keeps you stable on your feet and your shoe protected. Exclusively at Racquetball Warehouse, the sharply-designed Babolat Shadow Tour shoe is top-quality shoe from a top-quality brand.

FIT: Length = normal. Width = normal. Arch = Medium.

Features:

  • Non-marking Michelin Gum Rubber Outsole
  • DCS Technology for stability
  • Removable insole
  • Weight: 12.8 oz (Size 10.5)
